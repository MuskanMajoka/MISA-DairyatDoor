Fodder

Fodder is a very important part of dairy sector .Fodder is the feed that is given to livestock which is very important to maintain the health and the breeding efficiency of the livestock Fodder includes hay,straw,silage,rye,oils,mixed rations, sprouted grains,wheat.Also the waste material obtained after processing sugar beet can serve as fodder. Also fodder is also known as provender and some ingredients of animal origin are added to it.It is a coarse food for livestock composed of entire plants including leaves,stalks and grains, of such forages as grain and sorghum.
