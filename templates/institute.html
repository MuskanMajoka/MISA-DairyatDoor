Institutes working on dairy science

There are about 11 institutes at present which solely focus on dairy science and dairy industry while multiple agricultural colleges, veterinary and animal husbandry colleges also offer courses on dairy science. Among all these institutes, National Dairy Research Institute (Karnal and Bangalore) and the Sheth MC College of Dairy Science (Anand) have been identified as centres of excellence.

ICAR-Indian Council of Agricultural Research

The Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) is an organisation under the Department of Agricultural Research and Education (DARE), Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Government of India. Previously known as Imperial Council of Agricultural Research, it was established on 16 July 1929.
ICAR has 4 deemed universities under it namely
1. ICAR-Indian Agricultural Research Institute, New Delhi
2. ICAR-National Dairy Research Institute, Karnal
3. ICAR-Indian Veterinary Research Institute, Izatnagar
4. ICAR-Central Institute on Fisheries Education, Mumbai

NDRI-National Dairy Research Institute

NDRI is the first dairy science college established and it is a deemed university under the ICAR since 1989.
The NDRI works on different areas of

  • Dairy Production
  • Dairy Processing
  • Dairy Processing
  • Dairy Management
  • Human Resource Development

The Research and Development activities of NDRI focuses on three fundamental aspects of dairying

  • Management and production of dairy animals for better productivity
  • Innovating appropriate and satisfactory milk processing technologies and equipment
  • Informing the dairy farmers and entrepreneurs about the existing market demands and practical management input for making dairying a self-sustaining, profitable business

Sheth MC College of Dairy Science, Anand

Sheth M.C. College of Dairy Science, Anand, one of the foremost institutions for dairy technology education in the country, was established in the year 1961 as a part of the then Institute of Agriculture, Anand.
After elevation of the college as a Faculty in 1972 scopes of its activities have considerably expanded with the following mandate

  • Organize and conduct educational programmes both at undergraduate and post-graduate levels in various branches of Dairy Science and Technology in order to meet the manpower needs of technologists for managing dairy plants and subject matter specialists for research and teaching needs of the country
  • Organize short term specialized training programmes /refresher courses, etc. (both formal and informal) for dairy plant personnel, university teachers, research workers, small entrepreneurs, farmers and others in field of Dairy Processing

IVRI-Indian Veterinary Research Institute

The Indian Veterinary Research Institute (IVRI) is one of the premier research institutions dedicated to livestock research and development of the region, which was established in 1889.
The institute mandates

  • Basic and strategic research for improvement of animal health for enhanced productivity
  • Human resource development, imparting under-graduate and post-graduate education
  • Dissemination of livestock production and health technologies

The priority of the Institute in organizing research activities is developing suitable technologies that can be effectively utilized and also human resource development. The institute envisions to Harness veterinary and animal science research and education for sustainable growth in livestock sector so as to ensure food and income security.
