Veterinary

Veterinarians play indispensable roles in the dairy industry, serving as guardians of both animal health and overall herd productivity. One of their primary responsibilities is preventive healthcare, implementing vaccination programs to shield dairy cows from common diseases and conducting routine examinations to detect and address potential issues early on. In cases of illness, veterinarians diagnose and treat ailments, prescribing medications and offering advice on quarantine and biosecurity measures to prevent disease spread.
